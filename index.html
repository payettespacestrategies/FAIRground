White cells are inputs (SF, multipliers, cost pool, representative area/budget, and any ERPF % you pin); shaded cells compute. Baseline $/adj-SF = cost pool ÷ total adjusted SF (JAG Ch. 7.2); per-type $/SF = baseline × multiplier; ERPF % = $/SF × representative area ÷ representative budget. So the cost pool and space survey drive the percentages. The Detailed Option's ERPF line uses the type selected in the budget table.

How the $/SF basis works (JAG Ch. 7.2)

Aggregate annual facility cost (depreciation, utilities, O&M, leases); categorize research SF by type; apply the operating multiplier to get adjusted SF; divide cost by adjusted SF for a baseline $/adj-SF; multiply back out per type. That per-type $/SF is the unit rate; expressed against a representative budget it becomes the ERPF % in the lookup table.

JAG worked example: $5.0M ÷ 72,000 adj-SF = $69.44 baseline; wet $104.16 · dry $69.44 · clinical $83.33.

The two options. Detailed itemizes every ERPS element (RC, AMOR, RIDS as $; ERPF as a % of budget by space type) with GRO fixed at 15%. Simple bundles ERPF+RIDS into a flat 10% and adds GRO 15% (25% total support), with RC & AMOR still chargeable. Both solve with the JAG divisor method — percentages apply to the total budget, so total = direct / (1 minus ERPF% minus GRO%).

Cores as recharge. Vivarium per-diems, imaging, instrumentation and sequencing are fully-loaded per-use direct charges that fall under RPC in the RMR's three-category structure (the RMR doesn't enumerate a separate recharge line; the June 2025 doc and Uniform Guidance §200.468 do). Their square footage is excluded from the ERPF pool to avoid double recovery.

What this is not. A rate proposal. Percentages and multipliers are illustrative defaults from the RMR article reconciled with JAG (June 2025) and the July 15 deck; all are editable. Salary/budget caps, sub-awards, off-campus rates and forward-funding are out of scope.

Latest-model note. The June 2025 JAG document assumed GRO 10%; the July 15 deck and 2026 RMR article fix GRO at 15% and set the Simple bundle at 10%. Defaults here follow the later sources.
